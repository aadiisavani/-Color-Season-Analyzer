# -Color-Season-Analyzer
Upload a selfie with natural lighting, click three color points (skin, hair, eye), and instantly discover your seasonal color palette—Spring, Summer, Autumn, or Winter. Our algorithm analyzes your undertone, value, and contrast to generate a personalized 8-color palette. Download and share on TikTok &amp; Instagram. Completely free, no signup required.
